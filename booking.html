Lets Get You Booked In

Wether you are after a table for a few weekend drinks, a romantic dinner for two or a private party- We've got your covered.

Function Room Hire


The Greenbanks private dining and events space is perfect for all kinds of occasions and events, from wedding receptions and birthday parties to supper clubs, gigs & yoga.


With a range of capacities available to suit your needs, from 50-60 seated and 70-80 standing, the space offers free WiFi and P.A, benifits from its own private bar and The kitchen on request.

Restaurant Booking


Our Head Chef oversees our seasonal menus, focussing on fresh, local ingredients and suppliers. Vegan and gluten free options are always available so everyone can enjoy a great meal whether downstairs in the bar, or upstairs in our beautiful, relaxed dining room.


With a our provate dining room and a 70 seater restaurant we are capable of catering for groups of all sizes.
