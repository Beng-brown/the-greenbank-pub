Welcome to the Greenbank

An award-winning community pub in the heart of Easton


Perched on the edge of the historic Bristol Harbourside, The Ostrich sits proudly with its huge riverside beer garden and cosy interiors over two floors. Upstairs, the recently refurbished ‘Top Deck’ dining space and bar is available for private hire with stunning views over the harbour, while downstairs our main bar space is ideal for a quiet pint and a bite to eat.


We’re child-friendly, dog-friendly, and LGBTQIA+ friendly, with plenty of space for small and large groups, so everyone’s welcome at The Ostrich. Don’t forget to pay a visit to our ‘smugglers’ cave’ for a true slice of Bristol heritage! FINALIST ‘BEST NEW PUB’ – PUBLICAN AWARDS 2020
