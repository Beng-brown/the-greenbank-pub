Welcome to the Greenbank

An award-winning community pub in the heart of Easton


The Greenbank is charming and friendly free house, re-opened back in 2014. Over the past six years the pub has grown into a multi-award winning community hub for the people of Easton. From our incredibly varied events calendar to our selection of local tipples and vittles – The Greenbank is a unique and welcoming space, with a little bit of something to please everyone. A place to catch up with friends, colleagues & family. Wether in a cosy corner or in our sun-trap courtyard. Being apart of a local community is a key to who we are, so grab your nearest and dearest and we’ll see you at the bar!


We’re child-friendly, dog-friendly, with plenty of space for small and large groups, so everyone’s welcome- see you here!
